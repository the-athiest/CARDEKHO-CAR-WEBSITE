Financing FAQ

How does a used car loan work?

A used car loan allows you to finance the purchase of a pre-owned vehicle. Repay in monthly installments over an agreed tenure with interest. The lender may require documents like income proof and ID before approving the loan.

What are the primary benefits?

Used car loans offer easier ownership at lower prices, flexible tenure options, and competitive rates while maintaining your personal liquidity.

Can I prepay my car loan early?

Yes, most lenders allow full or partial prepayment of the loan before the tenure ends to reduce interest burden. Check your specific agreement for prepayment fees.

What is standard eligibility for salaried individuals?

Applicants generally must be between 21-60 years, have stable employment, and meet credit score thresholds. Salary slips, bank statements, and ID are required.
